We Specialize In Handcrafted Custom Cakes For Weddings,
Birthdays, Baby Showers, And Graduations In Toronto.

Velvet Lane Cakes is an artistry house specializing in handcrafted custom cakes for Weddings,
Birthdays, Baby Showers, and Graduations in Toronto. Our team of talented artists bring your
most loved items to life in elegant cake form so you can create lasting memories. We offer an
evolving dessert menu designed with a passion for art, quality and attention to detail. Our cakes
are works of art, almost too good to eat. Almost!
